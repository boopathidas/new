About Work From Farms - WFF


Work from Farms (WFF) concept attempts to add the missing elements to WFH (Work from Home)


- like going on a long drive
- enjoying the nature with family, friends and colleagues
- hanging out with friends, etc.


When you see a WFF banner in any farm, you can work from there.


Our mission is to bring people closer to nature and also help connect with the farmer community.

Take your family with you to add more fun to life.

So whenever you feel tired working from home, just pack your laptop and come to our farms to experience the wonderful change.

It is like moving your home + office to a farm.

In addition, you get an option to work in different farms.
